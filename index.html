About Me

Who Am I?

Hi! I'm Ryan. I am a full-stack developer, distinguished not only by my ability to manage both front-end and back-end as well as mobile applications, but also by my fast learning abilities, my mastery of programming principles and the clarity of the code. My career has been anchored in remote work for 3 years now. Beyond simple development, my expertise extends to design (for a year), where I create web and mobile interfaces.

Laravel and flutter are the cornerstones of my technical toolbox. I learned development with them, I grew up with them and I have since specialized in these areas to the point of even proposing libraries based on them or inspired by them as well as participating in the evolution of certain packages.

My dedication to code quality and maintainability propelled me this year to the role of Project Manager, where I focus on best practices (use of linter, respect for design patterns, respect for conventions, etc.).

My freelance solicitation is a testament to the exponential growth of my performance. Having worked with a wide range of clients, I have developed a talent for delivering satisfactory solutions that meet needs. This experience has not only honed my technical skills, but also my ability to think critically and strategically about product development.
